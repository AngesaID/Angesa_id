About Angesa.id

Angesa.id is an Indonesia writing improvement platforms that correct contextual spelling errors, check for common grammatical errors, provide alternative word equivalents, and provide citation suggestions

Angesa.id is a combination of two Sanskrit words, namely "Agem" which means Guidelines, "Basa" which means Language and the suffix ".id" is a domain name that aims as an identity for websites originating and intended for Indonesia.

Philosophically, this brand is dedicated to assisting Indonesians in writing appropriate and effective sentences based on the General Guidelines for Indonesian Spelling (PUEBI).
